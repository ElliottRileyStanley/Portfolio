Filler

Gamepidgeon is a app that allows users to play games over the messages app. One of these games is Filler, where 2 players have to compete to claim a larger area of a board. Each turn, players choose a color to make their corner, allowing them to spread across the board. I made a version of this game in TIBasic, running it on a TI-84 Calculator.
